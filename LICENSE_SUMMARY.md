# Smavg License Summary

This is a plain-language guide. The full binding terms are in [LICENSE](LICENSE).

Smavg uses the Smavg Modified MIT License, Version Genesis 1.0 2026.

## Simple Meaning

You can:

- use Smavg for free
- use Smavg commercially
- modify Smavg
- fork Smavg
- distribute Smavg
- sell products that include Smavg
- build companies using Smavg

You must:

- keep the license and copyright notices
- credit Smavg as the origin in public or commercial derivatives
- avoid pretending your fork is the original Smavg
- show prominent Smavg attribution only if your commercial product reaches the
  scale thresholds in the license

## No Automatic Fee At Scale

The commercial-scale thresholds do not mean:

```text
you must pay after this many users
```

They mean:

```text
if your product reaches major scale, show visible attribution to Smavg
```

The default commercial-scale requirement is attribution, not payment.

## Optional Commercial License

A separate commercial license is optional.

An organization may want one if it needs:

- attribution-free use
- dedicated support
- legal indemnity
- custom deployment terms
- enterprise procurement paperwork

Commercial terms are handled separately and are not required for ordinary use.

## Not Plain MIT

Smavg is MIT-style and permissive, but it is not plain MIT.

Use the full name:

```text

```
