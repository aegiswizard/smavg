This file summarizes commercial options for organizations that want separate
terms.

## When A Commercial License May Help

Companies may want a separate commercial license if they:

- prefer to remove the commercial-scale attribution requirement
- need legal indemnity
- want dedicated support
- want custom terms for large deployments
- want a long-term commercial relationship around Smavg

## Available Options

Commercial licenses are available for organizations that want attribution-free
use, dedicated support, legal terms, or custom deployment rights.

Commercial licensing is optional. Reaching a commercial-scale threshold under
the default license requires attribution, not automatic payment.

Commercial terms are scoped by deployment size, support needs, and usage model.

Options may include annual licensing, enterprise agreements, or usage-aligned
terms.

These commercial options are not part of the default project license unless a
separate written agreement is signed. The default license remains the Smavg
Modified MIT License, Version Genesis 1.0 2026.
