# Smavg 🐲 FAQ

## Is Smavg a backup tool?

Not first. Smavg is a repetition firewall and archive engine.

It can create archives, but users should still keep normal backups.

## Is Smavg just a compressor?

No. General compressors see bytes. Smavg tries to understand repeated file
families, repeated workflow setup, and AI context waste.

## Does Smavg delete my files?

No. Smavg does not silently delete files.

## Does Smavg upload my files?

No. The core runs locally.

## Does Smavg need internet?

No for the core.

## Does Smavg use AI to recreate exact files?

No. Exact restore is deterministic and hash-checked.

## What if Smavg cannot reduce my folder?

It reports a weak case honestly.

## What are tokens?

Tokens are pieces of text used by AI models.

Smavg token savings are Smavg-visible estimates, not provider billing-meter
numbers.

## How do I know the AI did not receive both raw and reduced data?

Smavg receipts record what Smavg supplied. They can prove Smavg supplied a
brief and exact expansions. They cannot prove what unrelated app history or
non-Smavg tools supplied.

## Can I restore exact files?

Yes. Exact restore is the core rule.

## Why show weak cases?

Because honest weak cases build trust.

Smavg should never pretend every folder is magic.
